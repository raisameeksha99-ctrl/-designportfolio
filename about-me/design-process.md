# My Design Process

> A human-centred, iterative approach to solving design problems.  
> Every project follows these five phases — looping back as needed.

---

## 1. 🔍 Empathize

**Goal:** Deeply understand the user and their context.

- Conduct stakeholder interviews to align on business goals.
- Perform user interviews & contextual inquiries.
- Build empathy maps and proto-personas.
- Review existing analytics and support data.

**Deliverables:** Research plan, interview notes, empathy maps.

---

## 2. 🎯 Define

**Goal:** Frame the right problem to solve.

- Synthesise research into affinity diagrams.
- Craft user personas grounded in real data.
- Map current-state user journeys.
- Write clear "How Might We" statements.
- Prioritise problems using impact/effort matrices.

**Deliverables:** Personas, journey maps, problem statements.

---

## 3. 💡 Ideate

**Goal:** Explore a wide range of solutions.

- Facilitate brainstorming sessions (Crazy 8s, mind mapping).
- Sketch low-fidelity concepts rapidly.
- Create user flows and information architecture.
- Evaluate ideas against user needs and business constraints.

**Deliverables:** Sketches, user flows, IA diagrams.

---

## 4. 🛠️ Prototype

**Goal:** Make ideas tangible and testable.

- Build low-fi wireframes to validate structure.
- Progress to high-fi mockups with visual design.
- Create interactive prototypes in Figma.
- Prepare test scenarios and task scripts.

**Deliverables:** Wireframes, high-fi mockups, interactive prototypes.

---

## 5. ✅ Test & Iterate

**Goal:** Validate with real users and refine.

- Conduct moderated usability testing (5–8 participants).
- Measure task success rate, time-on-task, and satisfaction.
- Document findings and prioritise fixes.
- Iterate on the design and re-test as needed.
- Hand off final specs to development with annotations.

**Deliverables:** Usability reports, iterated designs, dev-ready specs.

---

## Process Principles

| Principle               | What it means                                               |
|-------------------------|-------------------------------------------------------------|
| **User-first**          | Decisions are backed by user evidence, not assumptions.      |
| **Iterative**           | I loop between phases — design is never "done" in one pass. |
| **Collaborative**       | I work closely with PMs, devs, and stakeholders.            |
| **Data-informed**       | Qualitative + quantitative data guide every decision.        |
| **Accessible by default** | WCAG compliance is baked in, not bolted on.               |
